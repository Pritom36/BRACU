Section C – [General Math]

1. The salary of one person is 15,000 tk in December. In the next year he will get a promotion and his salary will be increased by 10%. What will be his salary in the next year? [ডিসেম্বর মাসে একজন লোকের মাসিক বেতন ছিল ১৫,০০০ টাকা। সামনের বছর, সে পদোন্নতি লাভ করবে এবং তার মাসিক বেতন ১০% বাড়বে। তাহলে সামনের বছর তার বেতন কত হবে?]

Options:

  • 16,500
  • 18,000
  • 15,500
  • 18,200

Solution: Current salary = 15,000 tk.

Increase in salary = 10% of 15,000 tk = \(\frac{10}{100} \times 15000 = 1500\) tk.

Salary in the next year = Current salary + Increase in salary = 15000 + 1500 = 16,500 tk.

Answer: a. 16,500

2. Saad spent 2500 tk on Eid shopping, 1000 tk on buying bus ticket and the remaining 65% he saved in his bank account. What is his total income? [সাদ ঈদের বাজারে ২৫০০ টাকা, বাসের টিকিট কেনার জন্য ১০০০ টাকা ব্যয় করল এবং বাকি থাকা ৬৫% নিজের ব্যাংক অ্যাকাউন্টে জমা করল। তার মোট ইনকাম কত?]

Options:

  • 20,000
  • 10,000
  • 15,000
  • 12,500

Solution: Total amount spent = 2500 tk (shopping) + 1000 tk (bus ticket) = 3500 tk.

The remaining amount saved is 65% of his total income.

Let the total income be \(x\) tk.

Amount saved = \(x - 3500\) tk.

According to the problem, \(x - 3500 = 0.65x\).

\(x - 0.65x = 3500\).

\(0.35x = 3500\).

\(x = \frac{3500}{0.35} = \frac{350000}{35} = 10000\) tk.

Answer: b. 10,000

3. If 2, 0 is a solution of the linear equation 2x + 3y = k, then the value of k is? [যদি 2, 0 হয় 2x + 3y = k সমীকরণের সমাধান, তবে k এর মান কত?]

Options:

  • 4
  • 6
  • 5
  • 2

Solution: If (2, 0) is a solution of the equation \(2x + 3y = k\), then substituting \(x = 2\) and \(y = 0\) into the equation will satisfy it.

\(2(2) + 3(0) = k\).

\(4 + 0 = k\).

\(k = 4\).

Answer: a. 4

4. If x : y = 3 : 5 and x - y = -2, then the value of x + y is?

Options:

  • 8
  • 2
  • 3
  • 5

Solution: Given \(x : y = 3 : 5\), we can write \(x = 3k\) and \(y = 5k\) for some constant \(k\).

Also given \(x - y = -2\).

Substituting the values of \(x\) and \(y\) in terms of \(k\): \(3k - 5k = -2\).

\(-2k = -2\).

\(k = 1\).

Now, find the values of \(x\) and \(y\): \(x = 3(1) = 3\) and \(y = 5(1) = 5\).

The value of \(x + y = 3 + 5 = 8\).

Answer: a. 8

5. Two trains for Mumbai leave Delhi at 6 am and 6:45 am and travel at 100 kmph and 136 km/h respectively. How many kilometers from Delhi will the two trains be together? [দুটি ট্রেন মুম্বাইয়ের উদ্দেশ্যে দিল্লি ছাড়ে সকাল ৬ টায় এবং ৬:৪৫ এ এবং যথাক্রমে ১০০ কিমি/ঘণ্টা এবং ১৩৬ কিমি/ঘণ্টা বেগে যাত্রা করে। দিল্লি থেকে কত কিলোমিটার দূরে ট্রেন দুটি একত্রিত হবে?]

Options:

  • 262.4 km
  • 260 km
  • 283.33 km
  • 275 km

Solution: The first train leaves at 6:00 am with a speed of 100 km/h.

The second train leaves at 6:45 am (45 minutes = \(\frac{45}{60} = \frac{3}{4}\) hour later) with a speed of 136 km/h.

When the second train starts, the first train has already traveled a distance of \(100 \times \frac{3}{4} = 75\) km.

Let \(t\) be the time in hours after the second train starts when the two trains meet.

Distance traveled by the first train from Delhi when they meet = \(75 + 100t\).

Distance traveled by the second train from Delhi when they meet = \(136t\).

For the trains to be together, these distances must be equal:

\(75 + 100t = 136t\).

\(75 = 136t - 100t\).

\(75 = 36t\).

\(t = \frac{75}{36} = \frac{25}{12}\) hours.

The distance from Delhi where they meet is the distance traveled by the second train:

Distance = \(136 \times \frac{25}{12} = \frac{34 \times 25}{3} = \frac{850}{3} = 283.33\) km (approximately).

Answer: c. 283.33 km

6. A man takes 6 hours 15 minutes in walking to a distance and riding back to starting place. He could walk both ways in 7 hours 45 minutes. The time taken by him to ride back both ways is: [একজন লোক একটি স্থানে হেঁটে যেতে ও সেখান থেকে ফেরায় আসতে ৬ ঘণ্টা ১৫ মিনিট সময় নেয়। সে যদি উভয় দিকে হেঁটে যায় তবে ৭ ঘণ্টা ৪৫ মিনিট সময় লাগে। আসা ও যাওয়া উভয় দিকে সাইকেলে যাওয়ার জন্য কত সময় লাগবে?]

Options:

  • 4 hours
  • 4 hours 30 min.
  • 4 hours 45 min.
  • 5 hours

Solution: Let \(t_w\) be the time taken to walk one way and \(t_r\) be the time taken to ride one way.

Given: \(t_w + t_r = 6\) hours 15 minutes = \(6 + \frac{15}{60} = 6 + 0.25 = 6.25\) hours.

Given: \(t_w + t_w = 2t_w = 7\) hours 45 minutes = \(7 + \frac{45}{60} = 7 + 0.75 = 7.75\) hours.

From the second equation, \(t_w = \frac{7.75}{2} = 3.875\) hours.

Substitute \(t_w\) in the first equation: \(3.875 + t_r = 6.25\).

\(t_r = 6.25 - 3.875 = 2.375\) hours.

Time taken to ride both ways = \(2t_r = 2 \times 2.375 = 4.75\) hours.

Convert 0.75 hours to minutes: \(0.75 \times 60 = 45\) minutes.

So, the time taken to ride both ways is 4 hours 45 minutes.

Answer: c. 4 hours 45 min.

7. If A = {2, 3, 5} and U be the set of prime factors of 210 then find A'. [যদি A = {2, 3, 5} এবং U হচ্ছে ২১০ এর মৌলিক গুণনীয়কের সেট, তাহলে A' নির্ণয় করো।]

Options:

  • {2, 3, 5}
  • {2, 3, 5, 7}
  • {7}
  • \(\emptyset\)

Solution: First, find the prime factors of 210.

\(210 = 2 \times 105 = 2 \times 3 \times 35 = 2 \times 3 \times 5 \times 7\).

So, the universal set U = {2, 3, 5, 7}.

Given set A = {2, 3, 5}.

The complement of A (A') is the set of all elements in U that are not in A.

\(A' = U - A = \{2, 3, 5, 7\} - \{2, 3, 5\} = \{7\}\).

Answer: c. {7}

8. A and B together can complete a work in 3 days. They start together but after 2 days, B left the work. If the work is completed after two more days, alone could do the work in? A এবং B একত্রে কোনো কাজ ৩ দিনে শেষ করতে পারে। তারা একত্রে কাজটি শুরু করে, কিন্তু ২ দিন পর B কাজটি ছেড়ে চলে যায়। যদি বাকি কাজ শেষ হতে ২ দিন প্রয়োজন হয়, তাহলে A একা পুরো কাজটি কত দিনে শেষ করতে পারবে?]

Options:

  • 5 days
  • 6 days
  • 9 days
  • 10 days

Solution: A and B together complete the work in 3 days, so in 1 day they complete \(\frac{1}{3}\) of the work.

In the first 2 days, A and B together complete \(2 \times \frac{1}{3} = \frac{2}{3}\) of the work.

The remaining work is \(1 - \frac{2}{3} = \frac{1}{3}\) of the work.

This remaining \(\frac{1}{3}\) of the work is completed by A alone in 2 days.

So, A alone can complete the whole work in \(2 \times 3 = 6\) days.

Let the number of days B alone takes to complete the work be \(x\).

Work done by A in 1 day = \(\frac{1}{6}\).

Work done by B in 1 day = \(\frac{1}{x}\).

Work done by A and B together in 1 day = \(\frac{1}{6} + \frac{1}{x}\).

We know that A and B together complete the work in 3 days, so \(\frac{1}{6} + \frac{1}{x} = \frac{1}{3}\).

\(\frac{1}{x} = \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{6} = \frac{2 - 1}{6} = \frac{1}{6}\).

Therefore, \(x = 6\) days.

Answer: b. 6 days

11. If the cost price of a dress is 7000 tk and the sell price is 5600 tk. What is the percentage loss? [যদি একটি জামার ক্রয়মূল্য ৭০০০ টাকা এবং বিক্রয়মূল্য ৫৬০০ টাকা হয়, তাহলে ক্ষতির শতকরা হার কত?]

Options:

  • 5%
  • 10%
  • 15%
  • 20%

Solution: Cost price (CP) = 7000 tk.

Sell price (SP) = 5600 tk.

Loss = CP - SP = 7000 - 5600 = 1400 tk.

Percentage loss = \(\frac{\text{Loss}}{\text{CP}} \times 100\% = \frac{1400}{7000} \times 100\% = \frac{14}{70} \times 100\% = \frac{1}{5} \times 100\% = 20\%\).

12. The degree of the polynomial, \(x^4 - x^2 + 2\) is? [প্রদত্ত বহুপদী সমীকরণের মাত্রা কত?]

Options:

  • 2
  • 4
  • 1
  • 0

Solution: The degree of a polynomial is the highest power of the variable in the polynomial. In the polynomial \(x^4 - x^2 + 2\), the terms are \(x^4\), \(-x^2\), and \(2\). The powers of \(x\) in these terms are 4, 2, and 0 (since \(2 = 2x^0\)). The highest power is 4.

Answer: b. 4

13. A box has 210 coins of denominations one-rupee and fifty paise only. The ratio of their respective values is 13:11. The number of one-rupee coin is [একটি বাক্সে এক টাকা ও ৫০ পয়সার মুদ্রা মিলিয়ে মোট ২১০ টি মুদ্রা আছে। তাদের মূল্যের অনুপাত ১৩:১১। তাহলে ১ টাকার মুদ্রার সংখ্যা কত?]

Options:

  • 65
  • 66
  • 77
  • 78

Solution: Let the number of one-rupee coins be \(n_1\) and the number of fifty-paise coins be \(n_{0.5}\).

Total number of coins: \(n_1 + n_{0.5} = 210\).

Value of one-rupee coins = \(1 \times n_1 = n_1\) rupees.

Value of fifty-paise coins = \(0.5 \times n_{0.5} = \frac{1}{2} n_{0.5}\) rupees.

Ratio of their values: \(\frac{n_1}{\frac{1}{2} n_{0.5}} = \frac{13}{11}\).

\(\frac{2n_1}{n_{0.5}} = \frac{13}{11} \implies n_{0.5} = \frac{22}{13} n_1\).

Substitute this into the total number of coins equation:

\(n_1 + \frac{22}{13} n_1 = 210\).

\(\frac{13n_1 + 22n_1}{13} = 210\).

\(\frac{35n_1}{13} = 210\).

\(n_1 = \frac{210 \times 13}{35} = 6 \times 13 = 78\).

The number of one-rupee coins is 78.

Answer: d. 78

14. The rectangular field has a perimeter of 480 meters and the ratio of its length to breadth is 5:3. What is the area of the field? [একটি আয়তাকার মাঠের পরিসীমা ৪৮০ মিটার এবং তার দৈর্ঘ্য ও প্রস্থের অনুপাত ৫:৩। মাঠের ক্ষেত্রফল কত?]

Options:

  • 7200 m\(^2\)
  • 15000 m\(^2\)
  • 13500 m\(^2\)
  • 54000 m\(^2\)

Solution: Let the length of the rectangular field be \(5x\) meters and the breadth be \(3x\) meters.

Perimeter = \(2(\text{length} + \text{breadth}) = 2(5x + 3x) = 2(8x) = 16x\) meters.

Given perimeter = 480 meters.

So, \(16x = 480 \implies x = \frac{480}{16} = 30\) meters.

Length = \(5x = 5 \times 30 = 150\) meters.

Breadth = \(3x = 3 \times 30 = 90\) meters.

Area of the field = Length × Breadth = \(150 \times 90 = 13500\) m\(^2\).

Answer: c. 13500 m\(^2\)

15. If a square field has a diagonal length of 16 meters, what is the area of the field? [একটি বর্গাকার মাঠের কর্ণের দৈর্ঘ্য ১৬ মিটার হলে, মাঠটির ক্ষেত্রফল কত?]

Options:

  • 126 m\(^2\)
  • 128 m\(^2\)
  • 130 m\(^2\)
  • 132 m\(^2\)

Solution: Let the side length of the square field be \(a\) meters.

The diagonal of a square is given by \(d = a\sqrt{2}\).

Given diagonal \(d = 16\) meters.

So, \(a\sqrt{2} = 16 \implies a = \frac{16}{\sqrt{2}} = \frac{16\sqrt{2}}{2} = 8\sqrt{2}\) meters.

The area of the square field is \(A = a^2 = (8\sqrt{2})^2 = 64 \times 2 = 128\) m\(^2\).

Answer: b. 128 m\(^2\)

01. Evaluate (যোগজীকরণ করো): \(\int \frac{1}{9 + x^2} \, dx\)

Options:

  • \(\frac{1}{3} \tan^{-1}(\frac{x}{3}) + c\)
  • \(3 \tan^{-1}(\frac{x}{3}) + c\)
  • \(\frac{1}{3} \sin^{-1}(\frac{x}{3}) + c\)
  • \(3 \sin^{-1}(\frac{x}{3}) + c\)

Solution:

We need to evaluate the integral \(\int \frac{1}{9 + x^2} \, dx\).

We know that \(\int \frac{1}{a^2 + x^2} \, dx = \frac{1}{a} \tan^{-1}(\frac{x}{a}) + c\).

Here, \(a^2 = 9\), so \(a = 3\).

Therefore, \(\int \frac{1}{9 + x^2} \, dx = \frac{1}{3} \tan^{-1}(\frac{x}{3}) + c\).

Answer: a. \(\frac{1}{3} \tan^{-1}(\frac{x}{3}) + c\)

02. What is the value of \(\frac{d}{dx} \cos(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)? ( \(\frac{d}{dx} \cos(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)-এর মান কোনটি?)

Options:

  • \(-\frac{\pi}{180} \sin(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)
  • \(\frac{\pi}{180} \sin(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)
  • \(\cos(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)
  • \(\sin(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)

Solution:

We need to find the derivative of \(y = \cos(\frac{\pi x}{180})\) with respect to \(x\).

Using the chain rule: \(\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{d}{dx} \cos(u) \cdot \frac{du}{dx}\), where \(u = \frac{\pi x}{180}\).

\(\frac{d}{dx} \cos(u) = -\sin(u)\).

\(\frac{du}{dx} = \frac{d}{dx} (\frac{\pi x}{180}) = \frac{\pi}{180}\).

Therefore, \(\frac{dy}{dx} = -\sin(\frac{\pi x}{180}) \cdot \frac{\pi}{180} = -\frac{\pi}{180} \sin(\frac{\pi x}{180})\).

Answer: a. \(-\frac{\pi}{180} \sin(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)

03. If \(\lim_{x \to 0} f(x) = 1\), then which one of the following is true for \(f(x)\) = ? [যদি \(\lim_{x \to 0} f(x) = 1\) হয়, তাহলে নিচের কোনটি \(f(x)\) = ?]

Options:

  • \(cos(x)\)
  • \(\frac{\sin(x)}{x}\)
  • \(\frac{\tan(x)}{x}\)
  • \(\frac{\tan(x)}{sin(x)}\)

Solution:

We need to evaluate the limit of each function as \(x \to 0\).

  • a. \(\lim_{x \to 0} \cos(x) = \cos(0) = 1\).
  • b. \(\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin(x)}{x} = 1\) (standard limit).
  • c. \(\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\tan(x)}{x} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin(x)}{x \cos(x)} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin(x)}{x} \cdot \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1}{\cos(x)} = 1 \cdot \frac{1}{1} = 1\).
  • d. \(\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\tan(x)}{\sin(x)} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin(x)/\cos(x)}{\sin(x)} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1}{\cos(x)} = \frac{1}{1} = 1\).

All the given functions have a limit of 1 as \(x \to 0\).

Answer: a. \(cos(x)\), b. \(\frac{\sin(x)}{x}\), c. \(\frac{\tan(x)}{x}\), d. \(\frac{\tan(x)}{sin(x)}\) (All options are correct)

04. What is the slope of the line joining the points (1, 2) and (3, 4)? [(1, 2) ও (3, 4) বিন্দুগামী সরলরেখার ঢাল কত?]

Options:

  • -1
  • 1
  • 0
  • 2

Solution:

The slope \(m\) of a line joining two points \((x_1, y_1)\) and \((x_2, y_2)\) is given by \(m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}\).

Here, \((x_1, y_1) = (1, 2)\) and \((x_2, y_2) = (3, 4)\).

So, \(m = \frac{4 - 2}{3 - 1} = \frac{2}{2} = 1\).

Answer: b. 1

05. Which of the following functions has an inverse that is also a function? [নিচের কোন ফাংশনটির বিপরীত ফাংশনও একটি ফাংশন?]

Options:

  • \(f(x) = x^2\)
  • \(f(x) = |x|\)
  • \(f(x) = 2x + 1\)
  • \(f(x) = 1/x\)

Solution:

A function has an inverse that is also a function if and only if it is one-to-one (injective), meaning that for every \(y\) in the range, there is exactly one \(x\) in the domain such that \(f(x) = y\). Graphically, this means the function passes the horizontal line test.

  • \(f(x) = x^2\) fails the horizontal line test (e.g., \(f(2) = 4\) and \(f(-2) = 4\)).
  • \(f(x) = |x|\) also fails the horizontal line test (e.g., \(f(2) = 2\) and \(f(-2) = 2\)).
  • \(f(x) = 2x + 1\) is a linear function with a non-zero slope, so it is one-to-one and has an inverse function \(f^{-1}(x) = \frac{x - 1}{2}\).
  • \(f(x) = 1/x\) is also one-to-one (its graph passes the horizontal line test) and its inverse is \(f^{-1}(x) = 1/x\).

Both \(f(x) = 2x + 1\) and \(f(x) = 1/x\) have inverses that are also functions.

Answer: c. \(f(x) = 2x + 1\) and d. \(f(x) = 1/x\) (Both options are correct)

06. At which temperature Celsius scale and Fahrenheit scale will show equal temperature? [কোন তাপমাত্রায় সেলসিয়াস স্কেল এবং ফারেনহাইট স্কেল সমান তাপমাত্রা দেখাবে?]

Options:

  • 40°
  • 60°
  • -60°
  • -40°

Solution:

The relationship between Celsius (\(C\)) and Fahrenheit (\(F\)) is given by the formula \(F = \frac{9}{5}C + 32\).

We want to find the temperature at which \(F = C\).

So, we set \(C = \frac{9}{5}C + 32\).

\(C - \frac{9}{5}C = 32\).

\(\frac{5C - 9C}{5} = 32\).

\(\frac{-4C}{5} = 32\).

\(-4C = 160\).

\(C = -40\).

Therefore, at -40°, Celsius and Fahrenheit scales show equal temperature.

Answer: d. -40°

09. A 2 kg object is lifted to a height of 5 meters. What is the potential energy gained by the object? (অভিকর্ষ বলের বিরুদ্ধে একটি ২ কেজি ভরের বস্তুকে ৫ মিটার উচ্চতায় তোলা হলো। বস্তু দ্বারা অর্জিত বিভব শক্তি কত?) (g = 9.8 m/s\(^2\))

Options:

  • 5 J
  • 10 J
  • 98 J
  • 49 J

Solution:

The potential energy (PE) gained by an object lifted to a height \(h\) is given by \(PE = mgh\), where \(m\) is the mass, \(g\) is the acceleration due to gravity, and \(h\) is the height.

Given: \(m = 2\) kg, \(h = 5\) m, \(g = 9.8 \, \text{m/s}^2\).

\(PE = 2 \times 9.8 \times 5 = 19.6 \times 5 = 98\) J.

Answer: c. 98 J

10. A ball of mass 0.5 kg is thrown upwards with a velocity of 10 m/s. What is the potential energy of the ball at its maximum height? (g = 10 m/s\(^2\)) [0.5 কেজি ভরের একটি বলকে 10 মি/সেকেন্ড বেগে উপরের দিকে নিক্ষেপ করা হয়। সর্বোচ্চ উচ্চতায় বলটির বিভব শক্তি কত?]

Options:

  • 25 J
  • 50 J
  • 100 J
  • 125 J

Solution:

At the maximum height, the kinetic energy of the ball becomes zero, and all the initial kinetic energy is converted into potential energy.

Initial kinetic energy (KE) = \(\frac{1}{2}mv^2\), where \(m\) is the mass and \(v\) is the initial velocity.

Given: \(m = 0.5\) kg, \(v = 10\) m/s.

\(KE = \frac{1}{2} \times 0.5 \times (10)^2 = \frac{1}{2} \times 0.5 \times 100 = 0.25 \times 100 = 25\) J.

At the maximum height, Potential Energy (PE) = Initial Kinetic Energy (KE) = 25 J.

Answer: a. 25 J
